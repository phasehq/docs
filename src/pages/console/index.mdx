# Console

The Phase Console is "Mission Control" for your application secrets. It is the single source of truth for application secrets that you manage through Phase.

The Phase Console has tools to manage all your Apps, collaborate with your team securely via end-to-end encryption and integrates seamlessly with the Phase CLI for local development, CI enviornments or full-fledged production infrastructure.
