# Overview

Explore deployment options for self hosting Phase

To meet various compliance requirements, you may want to self-host Phase instead of using [Phase Cloud](https://console.phase.dev). Self-hosted Phase Console allows you to maintain your sensitive information within your own infrastructure and network, ensuring complete control over your data. Choose from a variety of deployment options listed below to get started.
